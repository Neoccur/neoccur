What is neoccur?


neoccur is a website designed to let you meet new people with common interests. There then is a messaging service at your disposition to engage a conversation with these people.








How do you use neoccur?


neoccur is simple to use. When you sign up, you give your interests and skills. Then, you can search for people by their own skills or interests.








What are examples of things we can search for?


On neoccur, you can for example search for: a programming teacher, someone to help you writing stories, someone to collaborate with on projects, etc...
On neoccur, you can really search for anything, wether it is personal or professional.
